Coffee

Spiced Mocha Latte

This latte is made with rich espresso, creamy milk, and a blend of warm spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. The chocolatey flavor comes from high-quality cocoa powder, and the sweetness is balanced out with a touch of maple syrup. Our cocoa powder is sourced from a fair trade cooperative in Ghana that ensures ethical labor practices and sustainable farming methods.

Honey Lavender Latte

This latte is a delicate and floral twist on the classic latte. We use a shot of espresso and velvety steamed milk, flavored with a homemade honey-lavender syrup. The honey is sourced from a local apiary that prioritizes natural beekeeping practices, and the lavender is grown organically on a family-owned farm in France.

Iced Caramel Macchiato

This refreshing iced coffee is perfect for warmer weather. We start with a double shot of espresso, pour it over ice, and add a generous drizzle of creamy caramel sauce. The milk is lightly frothed and added on top, creating the classic "macchiato" layered effect. Our caramel sauce is made in-house with organic sugar and local cream from a nearby dairy.
