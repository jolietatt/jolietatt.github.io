About Brews and Blooms

Welcome to our coffee and plant shop! We're a group of friends who came together with a shared vision: to create a space where people could enjoy their favorite coffee drinks while being surrounded by lush greenery. We all love working in natural settings, but we also wanted the cozy and social atmosphere of a coffee shop. That's why we decided to start this business. We believe that coffee and plants are a perfect match and we're thrilled to be able to offer both to our customers. Our goal is to provide a warm and inviting space where you can relax and rejuvenate while surrounded by nature. We hope you enjoy your visit to our coffee and plant shop!

     

Come check out the many features of our little shop! Features include:

  • Mixes of your favorite drinks including coffee, tea, hot chocolate, and more
  • Delicious pastries homemade each morning
  • A wide collection of house plants
  • Classes taught once a month on houseplants and how to care for them
  • Plants accessories
